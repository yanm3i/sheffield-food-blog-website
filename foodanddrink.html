Food and Drink

This page describes some locations to buy food and drinks on campus.

The Diamond Kitchen

Diamond Kitchen on Google Maps
Source

In the Diamond Kitchen, they offer a range of meal deals, hot drinks and hot food. With plenty of seating areas, it's the perfect place to have a cosy lunch right after lectures.

Sheffield Students' Union

Students' Union on Google Maps
Source

In the University of Sheffield's Student Union, you can find a range of food choices from picking up some snacks from Our Shop, or to having some bubble tea or some pastries at Baked, all found within the student's union.

John's Van

John's Van on Google Maps
Source

Found right outside the University's Hicks Building is John's Van - an easy to-go pick for any students that want a quick snack or beverage inbetween their lectures.

With the homely vibe it gives to students, it frequents with long lines of eager students wanting freshly cooked burgers or wraps at their convenience.

Heartspace Cafe

Heartspace Cafe on Google Maps
Source

Located in the Frederick Mappin Building is the Heartspace Cafe, a large open space welcome to anyone wanting to have a nice lunch or a study session amongst the beautiful architecture.
